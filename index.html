06 — Strategic Recommendations

Five Actions.
$100K+ in Recoverable Profit.

Each recommendation is directly traceable to a specific data finding. Ordered by estimated profit impact.

1
Cap All Discounts at 20% — Immediately
Discounts above 20% are provably loss-making across every category and region. Implement a hard ceiling at 20% for all sales staff. Exceptions require senior approval with documented justification. Estimated annual profit recovery: $100K+.
● High Priority
2
Investigate & Reprice Furniture — Especially Tables
Furniture operates at 2.6% margin with Tables losing $17.8K annually. Conduct a cost-to-serve analysis. Options: increase base price 10–15%, remove discount eligibility, or discontinue the lowest-margin SKUs.
● High Priority
3
Double Down on Technology — Prioritise Copiers
Technology earns 17.4% margin. Copiers generate $56K profit at a 37% margin. Assign dedicated sales resources, build copier-specific campaigns, and develop maintenance and subscription bundles for repeat revenue.
● Medium Priority
4
Audit Discount Practices in Loss-Making States
Texas, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Illinois collectively lose $72K on $444K in sales. Compare their discount distributions to California and New York. Retrain regional sales managers on margin-aware selling practices.
● High Priority
5
Build a Q4 Revenue Maximisation Playbook
Q4 is the strongest quarter every single year. Begin inventory and staffing ramp-up in August. Run October–December campaigns that emphasise value over discounts — customers are already primed to buy without price incentives.
● Medium Priority
